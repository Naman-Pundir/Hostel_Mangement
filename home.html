About IIITA hostel facilities

Indian Institute of Information Technology, Allahabad is a fully residential campus and hence we provide topnotch hostel facilities to make our students feel like home.
In IIITA there are total of 5 hostels accomodating boys, and a total of 3 hostel accomodating girls. Each of them includes there own mess, gym, common room with table tennis and television. There is also full campus wifi and lan facilities.

The hostels have indoor badminton courts with floodlights as well as a night canteen. There are other recreational ways in the campus itself including all sorts of sports facilities, a cafeteria, general stores, medical healthcare centre etc. The hostels have full maintainance assistance and provide services regardin water-supply and electrical works are provided by the hostel council of wardens itself.
